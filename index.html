ABOUT US


The Panorama restaurant is located overlooking the Black Sea.


Wild fish, oysters, urchins, octopus shrimp, crabs and other delicacies — you will find all this and even more at the Panorama restaurant.


The indispensable accompaniment of an evening at the Panorama restaurant is wine; the collection of bottles includes over 40 copies of the Old and New Worlds, including selected sparkling wines from all over the world.
